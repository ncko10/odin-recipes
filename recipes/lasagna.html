Lovely Lasagna

Finished product

This is a favourite of mine. I'm sure you'll love it too.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 package lasagna sheets
  • 1 pound cottage cheese
  • 3/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 package shredded mozzarella cheese

Recipe

  1. Combine pork and beef mince in a pan over medium-high heat. Cook and stir until brown for 5-7 minutes
  2. Add onion and cook until translucent, about 5 mins.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce and seasonings. Reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer for 30 mins.
  4. While sauce is simmering, boil a large pot of salted water. Cook lasagna sheets until tender, 8-10 mins. While cooking, preheat oven to 190C
  5. Mix cottage cheese, parmesan cheese, eggs, salt and pepper in a large bowl.
  6. Assemble lasagna in a baking dish. Layer sauce then lasagna sheets twice.
  7. Add half the cheese mixture, then two more layers. Finish with remainder of cheese mixture. Cover with aluminium foil.
  8. Bake in the oven for 30-40 mins, then uncover and cook for 5-10 mins until golden brown.
  9. Remove from oven, sit for 10 mins, then enjoy!
